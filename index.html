About

Since May 2012 we've held monthly JavaScript talks. Today the group is 500 members strong and has a great friendly community that we've been proud to see grow.

Format

Talk nights are usually held on the last Wednesday of every month. They usual consist of two main talks and occasionally lightning talks that can be on any topic.

Fancy speaking?

Bristol JS is only as strong as it's speakers so we're always on the lookout for great talks. Drop us an e-mail with an outline of your talk as well as your availability, if it's right for us we'll get you booked in.
